<h1 align="center">
	Rock Paper Scissors
</h1>

**[Source Code](src/main.rs)**

Rock Paper Scissors is a guessing game. You and the computer will choose "Rock", "paper", or "scissors". Rock beats scissors. Paper beats rock. Scissors beats paper.

## Input

This game uses commands:
- `rock` or `r`
- `paper` or `p`
- `scissors` or `s`
- `exit` or `e`
